Health and Wellness Web Application

Capstone Project - Spring 2019

This is a responsive health and wellness website to manage corporate wellness. In this application, the doctor can review their patient’s health issues , behavior and fitness details; and also facilitates the doctor’s ability to coach the patients through online lectures and exercise videos. Also, the patients have access to the prescribed exercises and meal plans, and to log their blood pressure, heart rate and other fitness details from third party sensors.

The main goal of our project is to fix the bugs to facilitate end - end demo of the existing application. and to add HIPAA compliance for securely capturing patient’s details and documents.
